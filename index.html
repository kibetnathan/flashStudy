Why use Flash Study?

Flashcards for Active Recall:

The app automatically generates flashcards from your chosen topic. This feature encourages active recall, helping students strengthen memory and understanding by repeatedly retrieving key information rather than just rereading notes.

Short Quizzes for Self-Assessment:

After reviewing a topic, the app creates short quizzes to test comprehension. This lets learners immediately identify weak points, track progress, and reinforce what they’ve learned — making study sessions more focused and effective.

YouTube Integration for Deeper Understanding:

The app fetches relevant YouTube videos for the selected subject. This gives students quick access to visual explanations and real-world examples, supporting different learning styles and keeping studying engaging and dynamic.
